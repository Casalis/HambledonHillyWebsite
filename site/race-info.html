Race Info

Everything you need to know about courses, entry fees and race-day logistics for the Hambledon Hilly.

Courses

Five distances, all starting and finishing at race HQ within the Hambledon Vineyard Estate.

1km Entry £5.00 · Start 9.35am

Confined to footpaths and trails within the Hambledon Vineyard Estate. Well marshalled, and children may be accompanied by an adult at no extra cost. The race is timed, with trophies for first, second and third place boys and girls.

2km Entry £5.00 · Start 9.50am

Confined to footpaths and trails within the Hambledon Vineyard Estate. Well marshalled, and children may be accompanied by an adult at no extra cost. The race is timed, with trophies for first, second and third place boys and girls.

5km Entry £13.00 · Start 10.45am

Quiet lanes, beautiful trails and through the heart of Hambledon village, with the last part on the estate. Well marked and marshalled, with trophies for first, second and third place men and women.

10km Entry £18.00 · Start 10.30am

Quiet lanes, beautiful trails and through the heart of Hambledon village, with the last part on the estate. There's a water station halfway through the race. Well marked and marshalled, with trophies for first, second and third place men and women.

Trail 20km Entry £20.00 · Start 9.30am

For the trail purist, this is a self-sufficient and self-navigated run. Course markings are sparse and marshals will only be at busy road crossings. There's a mandatory kit list, to be confirmed closer to race day, which will include the route downloaded to a watch and/or phone, water and gear according to the weather. Trophies for first, second and third place men and women.

Optional event t-shirt: £10.00, available to buy during the entry process. Routes can be viewed on the Routes section of this website, and downloaded directly: 5k GPX, 10k GPX, Trail 20k GPX.

Transferring Your Entry

How to transfer your entry to someone else, should you need to.

  • Login to Racesignup
  • Against your entry, tap Transfer
  • Provide the email address of the person who is taking your place
  • They'll get an email with instructions on how to take up their place; you'll get an email with the transfer code (anyone with the transfer code can take the transfer)

Please note that you are responsible for any transaction between you and the person taking your place. Transfers must be completed one week before race day.
